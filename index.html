Audio

Aliso AV has got your back when it comes to audio. We provide professional audio for every event from a formal dance to the spookiest of Halloween parties. Audio services include a DJ upon request or simply a playlist.

Professional Hype

Aliso AV's top notch DJs know how to get a party started! Our DJs can create hype for any situation, bringing a party from 0 to rave-like in seconds.

Lighting

Aliso AV knows how to wow your partiers with our high quality lighting. Our lighting designers can sync all of our lighting fixtures to the current tune, really getting the dance floor going.

Special Effects

At Aliso AV, we know how to hype a crowd more than any other with our special effects. We use professional-grade CO2 cannons and guns to spray crowds with cooling gas. Our geisers provide a sweet stream of fog to go over a crowd, and it looks all the better with lights shining on it.

About

Mission Statement

Our mission is to deliver high-quality event experiences regardless of financial means. We are committed to providing reasonable cost options, ensuring that everyone can participate in and enjoy the excellence we offer. Through innovation, we aim to maintain the highest standards in our productions.

What We Do

Aliso AV is based out of Aliso Niguel High School's ASB media department in Aliso Viejo, CA. We serve our Aliso area schools and surrounding communities.

At Aliso AV, we specialize in creating unforgettable event experiences with our focus on crowd engagement and creative design. We provide complete event production services, working closely with clients to ensure every show is customized to their vision. Whether it’s a high-energy school dance or a carefully planned show, our goal is to elevate the atmosphere and leave a lasting impression.

Our team is dedicated to making each event one to remember, using a combination of lighting, audio, and special effects to get the crowd hyped and create the perfect vibe for any occasion. From concept to execution, we bring professional expertise to every project, making sure the event not only runs smoothly but keeps the energy up from start to finish.
